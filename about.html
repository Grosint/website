ABOUT GROSINT

Built in Bharat.
Built for Bharat.

Garud Research & Tech Private Limited is an intelligence infrastructure company. We build end-to-end sovereign platforms for India's defence and security ecosystem — from entry-level SMINT to AI-powered OSINT to enterprise fusion intelligence.

MISSION
"India's intelligence apparatus cannot run on foreign rails. Every query that crosses a border is a signal that has left the nation. We exist to close that gap — permanently."

Grosint is not a SaaS reseller. We are not a systems integrator wrapping foreign APIs. Every component — storage, compute, inference, enrichment — runs on Indian soil, under Indian law, operated by Indian personnel.

THE GARUD STORY

Why we built this

Garud — the mythic sovereign of the skies — sees everything. That is the standard we hold ourselves to.

01

The gap we saw

India's investigators, intelligence officers, and defence analysts were using tools built by foreign companies — tools that sent query results to overseas servers, stored sensitive targets in foreign databases, and charged in dollars for every lookup. The operational security implications were clear, and yet there was no Indian-built alternative at enterprise grade.

02

The iDEX moment

When we submitted Anveshak under iDEX ADITI 4.0 Problem Statement PS-18 (IAF), it validated what we already knew: the Indian Air Force needed exactly this — an AI-powered, locally-run OSINT platform that did not phone home. The recognition accelerated everything. It confirmed that sovereign intelligence infrastructure is not a niche idea — it is a strategic imperative.

03

The full stack

We did not stop at a single tool. We built the full intelligence stack — Grosint for identity resolution from phone and email, Anveshak for open-source intelligence and AI-assisted analysis, and Drishti for multi-domain fusion across maritime, aviation, financial, and social signals. Together they form the most complete sovereign intelligence suite built in India.

HOW WE OPERATE

Non-negotiable principles

These are not marketing claims. They are architectural constraints baked into every product we ship. If a feature violates any of these, it does not ship.

AIR-GAP
CAPABLE

No Foreign Cloud

Every query, every result, every model inference runs on Indian infrastructure. No data crosses international borders. Ever. Our deployment model supports fully air-gapped classified networks.

OPEN
WEIGHTS

Sovereign AI

All AI inference runs on open-weight models deployed on-premise. No OpenAI. No Anthropic. No cloud LLM API keys that log your queries and feed foreign training datasets. Local GPUs. Local models. Full control.

RBAC
AUDIT

Zero-Trust Architecture

Role-based access control, cryptographic audit trails, and attribute-based security classification on every record. Access is granted per query, per analyst, per classification level. No standing permissions.

MADE IN
BHARAT

Indian by Design

Designed for Indian data, Indian law, Indian operational doctrine. We do not adapt foreign products for Indian use. We build Indian products from first principles — for the specific constraints and requirements of Indian defence and intelligence operations.
