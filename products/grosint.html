// OPERATIONS

Field Use Cases

USE CASE 01

Counter-Intelligence

A suspect mobile number surfaces during a border incident. Grosint maps the number to a digital identity within seconds — registered services, aliases, travel patterns, and associated accounts — before the situation escalates.

USE CASE 02

Background Verification

Pre-clearance verification for sensitive postings. An email or phone number returns the subject's full digital footprint, enabling analysts to flag inconsistencies in declared identity before access is granted.

USE CASE 03

Investigative Support

A fraudulent procurement contact leaves only an email. Grosint traces it to a cluster of linked phone numbers, social accounts, and prior registered entities — building a prosecution-ready digital chain.
